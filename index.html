Project Overview

One view of receivables, collections and credit risk.

Working-capital teams track receivables, collections, payment behaviour and credit exposure across customers, industries and account managers — usually in disconnected reports that make the full picture hard to see.

This Power BI project consolidates those into a single analytics framework, so finance leadership can monitor DSO, overdue exposure and recovery performance in one place and act sooner.
